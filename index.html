What is Otis?

Enterprise-grade AI framework for spam detection with adversarial defense

Proactive adversarial testing with 6 attack vectors: character obfuscation, semantic shifting, prompt injection, multilingual mixing, encoding evasion, and homograph substitution.

Blue Team Defense

Real-time threat detection with automated remediation. Detects homographs, script mixing, encoding anomalies, injection patterns, and confidence anomalies.
